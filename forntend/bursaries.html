Bursaries

Elmvale District High School Bursary

Each year, we provide a $300 bursary to a graduating student pursuing post-secondary education related to horticulture, botany, or environmental studies.

Application: Available from your school counselor.

Deadline: May 15, 2025

District 16 Dr. Ives Bursary

Students are encouraged to also apply for the District 16 Dr. Raymond Ives Bursary awarded by our parent organization.

Application Link: Dr. Ives Bursary Facebook

Deadline: June 1, 2025
